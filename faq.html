FAQ

Q: What is your pricing for photography sessions?
A: Please visit our Pricing page or contact us for detailed information on our session pricing.

Q: How do I book a photography session with you?
A: You can book a session by filling out the booking form on our Contact page or by giving us a call.

Q: What should I wear for my photography session?
A: We recommend wearing solid colors and avoiding busy patterns. It's best to coordinate outfits among family members for a cohesive look.

Q: How long does a typical photography session last?
A: A typical session lasts about 1-2 hours, depending on the type of session and location.

Q: Can I bring props or accessories to my photography session?
A: Yes, you're welcome to bring props or accessories that are meaningful to you and will add personalization to your photos.
